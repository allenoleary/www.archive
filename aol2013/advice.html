Advice and Management

Website Manager

I have substantial experience running websites, from ecommerce start-ups to lumbering Research Councils. This means I have been though innumerable site rebuilds, corporate strategy sessions, comms away days - the lot.

  • Ecommerce and website manager for iwantoneofthose.com (2000-2003)
  • Web Manager for The Medical Research Council (2004-2008)
  • Digital and Online Media Manager for the Teacher Support Network (2011-2013)

Project Management

Being a Website Manager I have done an awful lot of in house Project Management by default. I have also project managed every one of my smaller design/builds. In the last couple of years have started to become more interested in it as a discipline and have worked twice (2011 and 2014) for The Disasters Emergency Committee (DEC) as their in-house PM for their web rebuild projects.

I have PRINCE2 foundation training - and know just how seriously to take it on web projects.

Consultancy and Workshops

As well as being the in-house digital expert for all the companies I have worked in, sometimes organisations get me in to talk about specific aspects of the digital picture.

  • The LSE Library got me in to run a User Experience boot camp day.
  • The Genome Project got me for a couple of sessions - one on moving to Drupal, the other on working with agencies.
