My Life at Massey

Aim

To explore narrative and imaging forms to create at site that communicates the ways the members of class feel about their life at massey.

or

To share our local knowledge with new students

or even

Show 'em how it is

Intended Audience

  • New entrants into the school of design
  • Users with ver5 browsers, 1024x768 screen, a good connection (Massey Speeds)

What to do

  • Draw up a rough map of Massey design school and and the University AND any close things that you find significant (such as a bus stop, the servo, a cafe whatever you find yourself returning to.
  • On the map provide a secondary layer that marks out the various zones and how you feel about them (for instance I might mark this classroom as 'the arena' and the staff area as 'sanctuary', but you might disagree!
  • Pick out any spots (lets start with five) you have insider knowledge of or a big attitude about and give them a name. I might pick out the area where the 'egg' competition was held a few weeks ago, or the coke machine which stole my money.
  • Write a paragraph, do a drawing or a cartoon or computer created image which in some way catches the essence of this spot. If you're doing a drawing, give it a short caption. Do this for 3 of the spots you selected. Be honest, be funny, be insightful... whatever you want really, as long as you have an honest connection with it.

We'll sort out how to string it all together when we see what we've got...
