Homework:

Watch an episode of Seinfeld or a similar sitcom like the Simpsons, Becker, Just Shoot Me etc. Don't watch an episode of a serial like Neighbours, Wildside etc.

As you watch the program, jot down the important events that happen, like:

    • George decides to do everything the opposite to what he normally does or
    • Jerry smashes his car or
    • Elaine steals the dummy from the shop window

After you've finished watching, go back through your list and pick out the ten (or so) most important events and underline them. Bring along your work next week.
