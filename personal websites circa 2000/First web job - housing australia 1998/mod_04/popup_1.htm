4. The Dominance of the Detached House

1. Residential Housing Stock

Single family dwellings including semi-detached and terraces account for another 7.3 per cent, leaving only 15 per cent of stock in the form of multi-family dwellings such as flats and apartments. This is much lower than in other advanced industrial countries.

The Australian house is large by world standards, and is getting larger. Paradoxically, as the house has increased in size, the block or lot has contracted and is now smaller than it was two decades ago. More houses are two storey, and there has been a change in the use of outdoor space.
