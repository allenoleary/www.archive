March 2000: Allen wins Swinburne Uni Postgraduate Award

Yes, someone thought the casino project was the best thing that happened in postgrad coursework at Swinburne in 1998. And they gave me some money too, which was nice!

Much respect to Lisa and Fran for getting me interested and providing encouragement along the way...

February 2000: My stage play 'Deviations' to be developed by Elbow Theatre

Elbow Theatre recently applied for some development money to spend a couple of weeks going through 'Deviations' with me, and were successful. Sometime later in the year I will go up to Canberra and spend two weeks with them on the script. At the conclusion of the workshop period there will be a staged reading (a requiremnt of the grant) and hopefully they will feel inspired enough to mount a production.

Elbow Theatre was started by Ian Sinclair and Ken Spiteri in 1998, and has done productions in Melbourne, Canberra and a Seson at B Sharp in Sydney. Ian has also worked in the UK, and tutors at Aust National University as well as directing. Ken is a friend of my good friend Stephen Bain - they met in Paris doing a course with Enrique Pardau...

January 2000: Allen escapes corporate world to teach and write

After a satisfying and productive six months with SAI, I've moved back from the brink of corporate respectability. Now I'm teaching again (sessional al VUT tafe and Swinburne Uni) in Electronic Design and Interactive Media - which just means web publishing.

My goal is now to complete a few drafts of some plays I have hanging around and do some of my own web projects and freelance work.
