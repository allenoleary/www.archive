Website Manager

I have substantial experience running websites, from lean ecommerce start-ups to lumbering Research Councils. Innumerable site rebuilds, corporate strategy sessions, comms away days - I've done the lot.

I have logged plenty of hours turning corporate word docs into websites as well as sitting in front of email systems, analytics engines and generally making Content Management Systems do my bidding.

  • Ecommerce and website manager for iwantoneofthose.com (2000-2003)
  • Web Manager for The Medical Research Council (2004-2008)
  • Digital and Online Media Manager for the Teacher Support Network (2011-2013)
