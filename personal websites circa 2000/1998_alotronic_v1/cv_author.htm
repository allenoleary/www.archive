Author


Experienced Author for Stage, Radio and WWW.

Since 1993 I have been writing stage and radio plays. Everything I have written has been performed or broadcast by professional companies. I have also been active in the area of script development and assessment.

I started writing for the WWW in 1997, and have started developing web sites on a freelance basis or for my own artistic and research ends. I often do the whole lot, from writing copy through to coding and image creation / optimisation.
