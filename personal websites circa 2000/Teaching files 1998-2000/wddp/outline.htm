Aims of Course:

Advanced Site Design Overview
Advanced Site Design builds on the skill set from Intro to Site Design. The emphasis is on an expanded concept of visual and information design as well as technical facility with industry standard tools such as the Macromedia suite (Dreamweaver, Fireworks and Flash).

Duration
A total of 45 hours as follows
City South Melbourne Campus
6 x 7 hour sessions and 1 x 3 hour session
(Saturday 9.00am-4.30pm), or

Outcomes
Upon completion of this subject you should be able to:

  • Produce commercial standard design documentation.
  • Construct and publish sites to a commercial standard using advanced tools such as Dreamweaver, Fireworks and Flash

Pre-requisites
To successfully complete this subject you should have either:

  1. Completed the Web Design and Development subject Intro to Site Design, or
  2. Be able to demonstrate (by folio and example) the following skills:
  • Experience using the World Wide Web
  • Competent visual design and information architecture skills.
  • HTML, CSS and JavaScript coding skills
  • Publishing (server management) skills

Assessment
Assessment will be broken into two parts:
Skills assessments (30% of total mark)
This section will consist of three (3) short problems of a primarily technical nature. They will have a due date of one (1) week and are to be completed between classes. They will be assessed as competent (C) or non-competent (NC) only for a percentage score of 10% each.
Major work assignments (70% of total mark)
There will be two (2) major work assignments for this subject. They will be provided as a detailed brief and assessed in accordance with the standard Assessment Guidelines 2000 policy document

Delivery schedule

  • Dreamweaver - site planning and management
  • Dreamweaver - formatting text and images
  • Dreamweaver - tables and layers
  • Dreamweaver - forms, frames, styles and templates
  • Dreamweaver - DHTML techniques
  • Intro to Flash - expanding the user interface
  • Flash as a navigation device - menus and buttons
  • Flash forms - form elements to match your design idea
  • Fireworks - imaging for the web
  • Fireworks - advanced graphic production
  • Fireworks - image slicing and exporting
  • Fireworks - advanced rollovers
