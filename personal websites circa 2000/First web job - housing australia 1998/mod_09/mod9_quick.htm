9. The Small Role of Community Housing

The corollary of the dominance of community housing provision in Australia is a small role for community housing. While the community housing sector in Australia is historically small its origins derive from the same factors which contributed to the development of social housing generally within the western world, that is, the activities of social reformers and philanthropic organizations concerned about the living conditions of the poor’ Community housing in Australia (unlike Europe) however, received little government sponsorship in Australia, until the 1980s. Since then it has steadily expanded although still small be overseas standards or relative to the public sector.
