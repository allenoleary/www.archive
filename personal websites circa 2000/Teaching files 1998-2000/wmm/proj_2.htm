Writing for Multimedia

Project 2

Brief: Do the basic planning for a transposition of the 'Company of Wolves' story into a Multimedia piece.

Aim: To generate the idea for the transposition and generate schematic and conceptual maps for the piece. Imagine that you are going to pitch this idea to a client/producer. This documentation and maps would represent your idea in the first meeting.

Delivery: A printed document, plus hand or computer drawn maps and supplementary development material like sketches and conceptual maps.

The document should contain the following:

Title and by-line for work. A sentence

Rationale (The who, why, what and where of the work) Several good Paragraphs

Conceptual Maps. The rough sketches you did in class, plus any further conceptual development you did. I expect to be able to trace your path from your initial mind map to the schematic map, and hopefully pick up extra information about the piece like where the role of the user came from.

Schematic Map(s). A finished schematic (one or more) of the flow of the work. This might be a sitemap, a multipath flow chart, a map of the virtual terrain of the work depending on the work. I expect to be able to clearly track the spatial and/or time-flow of the work from these diagrams (This is the most important bit as far as I am concerned!)

Extras. If you make any things like character sketches or write dialog along the way, include them as well (they can be very rough)

Assessment: I will be looking for the following things:

  • A well defined idea, clearly expressed though the Title and Rationale.
  • A well thought out and highly effective schematic map
  • The path you have taken to reach the idea (conceptual maps, sketches etc)

I will not be looking for:

  • Advanced presentation techniques or methods
  • Visual excellence (you can have it, but we wont mark it in fairness to others who may be less fortunate!).
