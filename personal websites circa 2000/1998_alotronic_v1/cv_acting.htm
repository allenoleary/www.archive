Acting & Other Work


Acting

From 1989-1996 I was a professional actor in NZ.

I was involved in many theatre productions, (including `A Dolls House', `Twelth Night', `The Comedy of Errors', ` `Unidentified Human Remains', all at Circa). several TV series (`Fallout', `Cover Story', `Mirror Mirror'), and radio productions (`Larnach Cast of Lies', `The portrait of the Artists Wife', `Galipolli').

Also involved in student assessments at NZ Drama School, many workshops, script assessments for Playmarket NZ, as well as appearing in short films, corporate videos, and TV commercials.
