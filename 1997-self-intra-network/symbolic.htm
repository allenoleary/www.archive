A Symbolic Prosthetic?

The Next Paradigm?

If we've had functional prosthetics, logical and informational prosthetics, then the next phase of development conceivable is symbolic prosthetics. That is, as the computer imitates the logic functions of the mind to the benefit of the symbolic function, so the symbolic prosthetic (neurator?) would imitate the symbolic manipulations of the mind to the benefit of the `parasymbolic' functions of self.

 

Growing rather than Making.

The practical attempts at construction of such an instrument are taking place; on the one hand `neural networks' endeavour to link computers in complex nets that can process input in massively parallel ways, on the other hand some people are working on letting a simple computer intelligence evolve into a higher intelligence. The reliance on the logical binary nature of the computer is probably an impediment to this goal; it's possible that such a thing might evolve from chemical/biological means - this may be the first prosthetic that we have to grow rather than make.

 

Towards the Spiritual Realm?

What's certain is that a symbolic prosthetic could allow us to see patterns of another dimension again beyond Chaos; perhaps they would allow us to see `paranormal' or spiritual experience in a different way?
