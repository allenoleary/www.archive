Electronic Design 2

Tutor: Allen O'Leary (alotronic@netspace.net.au)

Project One: Blue or Red (20% of final Assesment)

Due: In class on the 2nd of August

Aims :  

To use the knowledege gained through the last few classes in a practical manner, and match an idea or expression of an idea with an appropriate use of HTML.

Brief:

To make a simple website that presents an idea or explores a single concept.

Your provocation is BLUE or RED and you can explore it in any manner you wish, ie:

  • You can desrcribe or show what the colour means for you (say Sky Blue)
  • You can desrcribe or show what the music means to you
  • You can desrcribe or show what the anything blue or red related (like the sea or sky, anger)
  • You can desrcribe or show what the feeling means for you
  • You can tell a simple story that arises when you think of blue or red

Timeline:  

This project is due in class in week 4 (ie the 9th of August). No informal extensions will be granted for this exercise.

There will be time to work on this in class on in week three, so if you get into deep problems, don't panic, first of all visit webmonkey (look under Authoring) to see if you can find a solution, then email me (alotronic@netspace.net.au) and we'll see what we can do...

Requirements:  

  • Project to be handed in on a single floppy (PC or Mac)
  • Make 4-5 HTML pages that link together in an interesting way
  • Use images (at least one per page) and words (your own or someone elses) to express your idea or feeling. Make sure you use at least one image as a link
  • You can use borrowed images if you like, but it would be nice to include one of your own
  • Use ABSOLUTE and RELATIVE links in your site (ie include links to external sites)
  • Use examples of the following types of tags: images, lists, font
  • Use html to control text, image and page layout
  • Give each page a title

Important points:  

    • Remember to name you files in lower case and leave no spaces in file names.
    • Keep all your images and pages in the same folder for now
    • name the first page of your site index.htm so I know where to begin...

Assesment:  

I will be looking for the following things:

  • Correct placement of tags, tag mirroring etc
  • A bit of creative flair, both in using html and in exploring the concept
