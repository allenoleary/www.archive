Project  
     
 

'Blue'

First Simple WebSite on Floppy Disc (PC or Mac)

 
     
aims :  

To use the knowledege gained through the last few classes in a practical manner, and match an idea or expression of an idea with an appropriate use of HTML.

  
goal :

To make a simple website that presents an idea or explores a single concept.

  • Your provocation is BLUE and you can explore it in any manner you wish, ie:
  • You can desrcribe or show what the colour means for you (say Sky Blue)
  • You can desrcribe or show what the music means to you
  • You can desrcribe or show what the anything blue related (like the sea or sky)
  • You can desrcribe or show what the feeling means for you
  • You can tell a simple story that arises when you think of blue
  
timeline :  

This project is Due this Tuesday (ie in five days!). No informal extensions will be granted for this exercise.

There will be time to work on this in class on Tuesday, so if you get into deep problems, don't panic, first of all visit webmonkey (look under Authoring) to see if you can find a solution, then email me (alotronic@netspace.net.au) and we'll see what we can do...

  
tech requirements :  

Make 3-5 HTML pages that link together in an interesting way

  • Use images (at least one per page) and words (your own or someone elses) to express your idea or feeling. Make sure you use at least one image as a link
  • You can use borrowed images if you like, but it would be nice to include one of your own
  • Use ABSOLUTE and RELATIVE links in your site (ie include links to external sites)
  • Use html to control text, image and page layout
  • Give each page a title
  • You may want to include lists, horizontal rules

YOU SHOULDN'T SPEND MORE THAN 3 HOURS ON THIS PROJECT. IF YOU FIND IT'S TAKING TOO MUCH TIME, SIMPLIFY.

 

  
important points :  
  • Remember to name you files in lower case
  • Keep all your images and pages in the same folder for now
  • name the first page of your site index.htm so I know where to begin...
  
assesment :   I will be looking for the following things:
  • Correct placement of <html> <head> and <body>
  • Links that work!
  • Use of html simple tables to control where images and text appear in relationship to each other
  • A bit of creative flair
