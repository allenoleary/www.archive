Meta Tags

The Idea

Meta tags appear in the head of the document and holdadditional information about the document, particularly what it's about. It is this information which is used by search engines to catalog and present your site in their databases.

The Basics

Your two basic <meta> tags look like this:

<head>
<title>Big Al's Pet Shop</title>

<meta name="description" content="Visit Big Al's Pet Shop in Melbourne, Australia, for all your pet care needs. We specialise in strange chewy things for your dog.">

<meta name="keywords" content="Bigs Al's Pet Shop, Pet Shop, Pet Care, Dogs, Cats, Pet toys, Canaries, Big Al">

</head>
