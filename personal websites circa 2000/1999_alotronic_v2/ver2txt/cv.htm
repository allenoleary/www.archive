Writing

Brief: I have an indepth understanding of the theory and practice of many forms of writing: An experienced and professionally performed playwright and radio dramatist, I also work creatively in hypertext as well as copy writing for web sites. I have also had some poetry published.

I graduated from Swinburne Uni with a Graduate Diploma in Writing in 1998 and also won the Postgraduate Course Work Award in that year for my writing project.

Plays:

  • Deviations. Currently in development with Elbow Theatre Canberra. Read at Downstage and La Mama.
  • Ride on Time. Radio Comedy currently being produced by Stephen Danby for RadioNZ.
  • The Vampyre Dances. Selected for Ocenia Playwriting Conference 1995, performed at Circe Theatre 1996, Murray Lynch directing.
  • Weightless. Radio Drama broadcast 1995 Radio NZ.

For more details about my playwriting, see writing.

Acting

Brief: I acted full-time from 1988 to 1996, for 6 of those years as a professional. After graduating from the NZ Drama School in 1991, I went on to play in numerous theatre productions, TV series and a few films.

Some selected highlights:

  • Lead in my own short film (written with Stephen Bain) 'Taking a Bath', shown Wellington and Auckland Film Festivals, Eat Carpet SBS.
  • Lead in 'The Betrayal' a dramatised documentary about WWII. Filmed in Warsaw. Shown SBS and TVNZ.
  • Krogstad in 'A Dolls House' at Circa in 1995, Colin McColls multi-award winning production.
  • Lead in 'Fallout' a mini-series by South Pacific TV.
  • Feste in 'Twelth Night' at Circa Theatre, Murray Lynch directing.
  • I played all the men in 'The Big Blue Planet Earth Show' winner of a Critics Choice award at the Adelaide fringe.
  • Lead in 'Larnach, Castle of Lies' winner of Best Radio Drama 1996.
  • Other notable productions/screen credits: The Frightners, Unidentified Human Remains and the True Nature of Love, The Comedy of Errors, The Learners Stand, Mirror Mirror, Cover Story.
