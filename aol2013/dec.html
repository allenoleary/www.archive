DEC - Project Manager

I worked with the DEC as a freelance client-side Project Manager on a major Drupal build.

2011

The Brief

The DEC were replacing their dodgy old site with a new Drupal build. They selected me to operate between themselves and their digital design and build agency. My job was to get good value for money and to advise the DEC's communication manager on digital as this was his first web build.

So what did I do?

Provided day to day liason between the DEC and their Agency, reported on progress, trouble-shooted both ways and generally advised the DEC on what the agency would expect and when, and what 'reasonable scope changes' might mean.

Did it work?

Very well. I was able to effectively communicate between the agency and the DEC and keep things on an even keel.

High performance

A unique feature of this site was that it is hosted on the Acquia cloud service so it can be 'spun up' on larger services with a couple of days notice - critical for the DEC as they take millions of pounds in a couple of days when an appeal is launched.

High value

Since it was launched the website has remained available and has coped with massive traffic spikes. When Simon Cowell tweeted their cause the DEC site kept up!

The website is a key part of the fundraising of the DEC and has many tens of millions of pounds put through it in the last few years.

2014

The Return

I am happy to say that the DEC invited me back again to be the web project manager on thier new, very ambitious website project. This time they are embracing mobile and social and suiting their site to the very much busier and more sophisticated digital landscape of today.
