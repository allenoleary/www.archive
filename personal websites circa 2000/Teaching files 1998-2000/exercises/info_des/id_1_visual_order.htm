Analyzing Information and Visual Order

Information design is nothing new. The Novel or enclyclopedia have very resolved and conventional information designs, as do posters or flyers. (We'll have a look at those in class)

New Media is beginning to have some conventions of it's own (top banner, navigation on the left-hand side). These conventions are often a cross over between older print media and newer media like film or television. Also you see more old media imitating new media (print ads that use the windows metaphor for instance)
