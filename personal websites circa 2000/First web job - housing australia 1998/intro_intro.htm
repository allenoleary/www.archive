Introduction:

This is an on-line information guide (and induction course) to Australia housing.

It written with two audiences in mind:

  • Those seeking general information about the attributes of the Australia housing system

  • Social Housing workers, particularly those in the community sector, requiring more knowledge of their work context

The objective is three fold:

  • To provide broad knowledge about the Australian Housing system

  • To provide knowledge about Australian housing Policy

  • To provide an induction course on housing to improve knowledge and decision makes skills particularly in social housing

Readers can:

  • Get basic data

  • Find out about policy

  • Locate housing in a national context

  • Examine particular housing tenures, issues and problems

  • Be referred to appropriate literature and websites for even more information.
