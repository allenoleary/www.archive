Copy for the Screen

In General

- Resolution, reading speed, drag, width
- Inverted pyramid, start with the conclusion
- Visuals reinforced with text and vice versa
- 80/20?
- And you can still be creative...

Headings & Link text

- Cut the cute and go for information
- Important words first
- Always rewrite them from print
- Don't tease the user, they don't care!
- 'Click here'

Page titles

- 40 characters, 6 words
- A new title for every page
- Reflect the information order
- Billboard style

Copy

- Cut at least 25%, preferably 50% of word count
- Increase paragraph breaks
- Bullet absolutely everything you can
- Make text active and add attitude
- Seven points a time
- Farm out detail to new pages
