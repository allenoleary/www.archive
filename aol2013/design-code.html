Visual Design & Code

Since 1997

Initially I learned how to make websites out of a very large book. There were no courses and not many people around to help. The internet was dial-up, there weren't any online data bases, it was a 'find-and-replace' world.

My first paid work was knocking up websites and flash animations for the University I studied writing at, then a very early digital agency.

    This is a very early animation I designed and made in Flash - 1998 and it still plays!

    A very early homepage for my personal site - it was the nineties...

In the late nineties I wrote and delivered some of the earliest web design courses in Australia - which later turned into this course.

Design

Thankfully things have moved on a lot since then. Sometimes things got easier (with content management systems) then they get harder again (ecommerce) then easier (blogging) then harder (mobile). It's a world that I enjoy and excel in. Web design now exists at an intersection between techncial, creative and business skills. You have to understand the buiness as much as html and css and psychology to deliver a good site.
