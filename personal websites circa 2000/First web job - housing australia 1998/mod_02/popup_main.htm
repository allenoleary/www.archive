2. Home Ownership in Australia

By contrast with other countries, owner occupied housing in Australia has been long established. In the late 19th century, close to half of all Australians were home owners at a time when few other countries had levels much above 20 per cent. Even in the 1980s Australia still had ownership levels higher than most other countries. Table 1 shows the trend in ownership over time.
