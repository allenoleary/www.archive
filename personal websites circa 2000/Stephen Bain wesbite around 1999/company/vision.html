The company, created in 1995 by Stephen Bain (NZ) and France Herve (FRANCE), is supported through project funding by Creative New Zealand (the Arts Council of NZ) and individual theatre commissions, and have received critics awards or nominations for each of their creations.

The company has opened Choreographic Theatre classes in performance arts schools, giving regular workshops in NZ and internationally, while Art Gallery and Urban Environments also set the scene for various performances and 'happenings', outside the formal theatre context.

Under Lili's Balcony initiated and participates in performance research workshops (including a residency exchange programme) with other companies and artists in the pursuit of developing and exploring many aspects of theatre. They welcome all Enquires into this area of meeting and research.
