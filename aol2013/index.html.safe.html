Since 1998 I have been designing, managing, advising on and project managing website projects and digital comms, from the very small client to the very large. See my complete CV for the blow by blow.

Most of what I do falls into one of three areas:

Digicomms & Management

I have been doing digicomms for organisations for many years. This includes work around email, going mobile, social media and project management.

IA, UX, Content Strategy

Contributing to larger projects with all that user centred design & content goodness. From card sorts to user testing and building prototypes in Axure or Drupal, I can get your user journeys, layouts and architecture aligned.

End to end design & builds

I also provide and end to end website service. This includes everything from advice, to graphic design to coding and deploying your site in Drupal. See showcase.
