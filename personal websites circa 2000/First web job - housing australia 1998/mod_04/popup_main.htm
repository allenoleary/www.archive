4. The Dominance of the Detached House

Most other countries, at a similar stage of economic and social development, have a greater mix of residential building forms than that of Australia which is dominated by the detached house (77.7 per cent of all dwellings) There are, however, variations from city to city with Sydney having a lower proportion than other capital cities.
