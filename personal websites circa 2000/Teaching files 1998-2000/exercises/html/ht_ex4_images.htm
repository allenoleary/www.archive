Exercise 4: Images out of Photoshop

1. Make two images in Photoshop - one photographic in nature, the other sharp and 'flat'.

2. Export both images in the following formats:

gif with a web palette
gif with an adaptive palette
jpeg at a compression of 10
jpeg at a compression of 8
jpeg at a compression of 3

3. Put all the images (10 altogether) into a page

4. Compare the images at various qualities and note their size
