SALT - Somewhere Alotronic Laboratory Theatre.

Salt is a resource based website for keeping track of my own interest in `Theatre in the age of the intelligent machine'. The design is done, but the content is fairly minimal!

It's a relatively simple site and attractive site. I wanted it to be broadly available, but still professional looking. I have made a nice little program in VB6 that takes care of keeping it all up to date and alphabetically sorted...
